in France and owned a restaurant in New York. He 
has published 14 books and has won six James Beard 
Awards.
nicole plue  is the pastry chef at Redd in San Francisco. 
She won the James Beard Award for Outstanding Pastry 
Chef in 2010. Her desserts have been featured in Gour-
met, Fine Cooking, Food Arts,  and Food and Wine.
Mikey price  is executive chef at Market T able restau-
rant in New York City.
Jeanne Quan  is a San Francisco Bay-area retailer and 
cooking teacher.
peter Reinhart  is the author of eight books on bread 
baking, including Peter Reinhart’s Artisan Breads Every 
Day.  He is the Chef on Assignment at Johnson & Wales 
University in Charlotte, North Carolina.
tony Rosenfeld , a Fine Cooking  contributing editor, is 
also a food writer and restaurant owner in the Boston 
area. His second cookbook, Sear, Sauce, and Serve,  was 
published in spring 2011.
Samantha Seneviratne  is former associate food 
editor and food stylist at Fine Cooking.  
Maria Helm Sinskey  is a noted chef, cookbook author, 
and Culinary Director at her family’s winery, Robert 
Sinskey Vineyards, in Napa Valley, California. Her most 
recent book, Family Meals,  was a 2010 IACP Cookbook 
Award winner.
paul Speck  is a winemaker in Ontario, Canada.
Molly Stevens  is a contributing editor to Fine Cooking . 
She won the IACP Cooking T eacher of the Year Award 
in 2006. Her book All About Braising won James Beard 
and IACP awards.
Alan tardi  began his cooking career at Chanterelle and 
Lafayette, with Jean-Georges Vongerichten. He was 
chef-owner of Follonico for almost 10 years. His latest 
book is Romancing the Vine .
nancy verde Barr  was executive chef to Julia Child 
from 1980 to 1998 and the culinary producer for PBS’s 
“Baking with Julia” and ABC’s “Good Morning America.“ 
She has written for many national food magazines and 
is the author of three award-winning cookbooks.Bruce Weinstein  and Mark Scarbrough  are the 
award-winning authors of 19 cookbooks, contributing 
editors to Eating Well,  and columnists for www.weight 
watchers.com.
Joanne Weir , a cooking teacher in the Bay Area of 
California, is an award-winning cookbook author and 
the host of “Weir Cooking in the Wine Country” on 
public television. 
Alan tangren , a food writer, was a pastry chef at Chez 
Panisse and collaborated on Chez Panisse Fruit with 
Alice Waters.
David tanis  is head chef at Chez Panisse, as well as the 
author of  A Platter of Figs and Other Recipes.
Rosina tinari Wilson , a contributing editor for Fine 
Cooking,  is a food consultant, teacher, and writer in 
Kensington, California. She is also the author of 
Seafood, Pasta & Noodles.
paula Wolfert  is the award-winning author of five 
cookbooks, including Couscous and Other Good Food 
From Morocco, The Cooking of Southwest France,  and 
Cooking of the Eastern Mediterranean.
Clifford Wright  is an award-winning cookbook author 
as well as a contributor to www.zesterdaily.com. He 
writes for many national food magazines.
